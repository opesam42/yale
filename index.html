Explore our courses

Fine Art
Equip yourself with the skills and experience to succeed in the creative economy. Fulfil your creative potential and ambitions in Fine Art.
Graphic Design
Explore the power of visual communication. Dive into typography, motion graphics, and interactive design to create compelling, impactful visuals.
Painting/Printmaking
Master the craft of expression. From figure drawing to experimental printmaking, explore techniques that bring your creative vision to life.
Photography
Capture the world through your lens. Develop your unique visual voice in both analog and digital photography, pushing the boundaries of the medium.
Sculpture
Transform ideas into physical forms. Learn the art of sculpting with diverse materials, from wood to metal, and explore contemporary themes.
Film Making
Bring stories to life through motion. Study documentary filmmaking, animation, and digital video to craft engaging narratives that resonate.

Research

Research at Yale School of Art explores new artistic frontiers, merging creativity with critical inquiry. Our faculty and students engage in diverse practices from digital technologies to social activism norms and contributing to cultural conversations. Supported by Yale’s vast resources, our research drives innovation and deepens understanding of the world.
